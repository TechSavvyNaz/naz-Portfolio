Hotel Management System

Description: The Hotel Management System is designed to streamline hotel operations, making it easier for hotel staff to manage room bookings, customer information, billing, and overall administration. It provides an intuitive interface and automates tasks, ensuring a seamless experience for both guests and hotel operators.

Features include:

  • Room reservations and booking management
  • Billing and invoice generation
  • Customer data management
  • Room availability and occupancy tracking
