Women Safety Website

Description: The Women Safety Website is a platform focused on promoting women's safety through accessible resources, emergency contacts, and educational articles. The site offers location-based alerts and reporting tools to assist women in emergencies. This project aims to empower women by providing essential tools to ensure their safety.

Features include:

  • Real-time emergency alerts
  • Location-based reporting tools
  • Articles and resources on safety tips
  • Emergency contact and support links
